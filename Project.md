Blood Bank Management System

1. Project Title

Blood Bank Management System

2. Problem Statement

Blood banks play a vital role in healthcare by providing blood for emergencies, surgeries, and medical treatments. Traditional methods of managing donor records, blood stock, and blood requests are often manual, time-consuming, and prone to errors. There is a need for an efficient system that can manage donor information, track blood inventory, handle blood requests, and provide real-time updates on blood availability. The Blood Bank Management System aims to automate these processes and improve the efficiency and reliability of blood bank operations.

3. Project Objectives

- To maintain donor information in a centralized database.
- To manage blood collection, storage, and distribution efficiently.
- To track blood inventory and monitor blood stock levels.
- To facilitate quick searching of available blood groups.
- To manage blood requests from hospitals and patients.
- To generate reports related to donors, blood stock, and transactions.
- To reduce manual work and improve data accuracy.
- To provide real-time updates on blood availability.
